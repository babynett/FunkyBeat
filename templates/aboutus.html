OUR SERVICES
Artists Management President & Managing Director

We provide professional representation and strategic guidance of artists, helping them navigate their careers by overseeing business aspects such as bookings, promotions, contracts, and overall career development.

Event Management Vice President & Musical Director

Provide planning, coordination, and execution of various aspects involved in organizing and producing music-related events. This includes concerts, festivals, gigs, and other live music performances.

Musical Scoring for Films & AVP President & Manging Director

We create and compose music specifically tailored to enhance the impact and storytelling of visual content. This process includes composing original music and synchronizing elements with the timing and mood of the visuals.




Musical Direction for Theater and Concerts President & Managing Director

Involves overseeing the musical aspects of a live performance. Rehearsing musicians, working with the cast or performers to achieve musical cohesion, and ensuring that the musical elements enhance the overall production.

Musical Arranging and Composition Vice President & Musical Director

Composing music for commercial purposes, such as advertising, film, television, or other media. This could involve adapting a piece for a different instrument or creating a new arrangement of a song.
