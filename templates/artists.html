BRASS PAS PAS

Meet BRASS PAS PAS, A 10-piece big band that came together in 2012 that is built around its members' common love for original Filipino music. They released their debut album "Greatest Hits" back in 2016 presenting a new twist to the classic Manila Sound. Playing along the genres of soul, funk, jazz and disco music.

10 AM DEPARTURE

Meet 10AM DEPARTURE, A 4 piece alternative rock band from Manila. This band is known for their electrifying live performances. They released their first single “ 2 caught up” back in 2019 and their debut EP “Pack of make believes” in 2021.

UNCLE BOB’S
FUNKY SEVEN
CLUB

Meet UNCLE BOB'S FUNKY SEVEN CLUB, Uncle Bob's Funky Seven Club is a seven-member band formed in mid-2022 and, in effect, born seven decades after the birth of funk and their fun namesake of sorts, Uncle Bob's Funky Seven Club is merrily comprised by musical director-bassist Francis De Veyra, vocalists Pael Gutierrez, Zsaris Mendioro and Faye Yupano, guitarist Gabe Dandan, saxophonist-flutist Nicole Tejedor-Reluya and drummer John Reluya.

SANSETTE

Meet SANSETTE, Hailing from Cebu City, Philippines, Sansette is a 7-piece surf pop ensemble that infuses sun-soaked melodies with retro and jazzy vibes, capturing the carefree spirit of city-island life. Rooted in the heart of the Philippines, their unique fusion of genres offers an irresistible sonic journey that celebrates the essence of coastal living. Join this musical adventure and embrace the Island Vibe as they ride the waves of their distinctive sound.

LILI

Meet LILI, Lili is an indie pop singer-songwriter from Metro Manila, Philippines. She launched her solo career in 2021 after several years playing keyboard and back-up vocals for her band, The Ransom Collective.

SPACE DOG SPACE CAT

Meet SPACE DOG SPACE CAT, What started out as an excuse for Jam Lorenzo and RJ Mabilin to play music with an ever-revolving cast of friends is now spacedog spacecat. Featuring fellow Manila scene veterans Jerros Dolino, Marc Inting, Evee Simon, and Janine Samaniego, the band plays indie rock inspired by slackerdom and a constant longing for the beach.
